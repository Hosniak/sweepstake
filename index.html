📋 Sweepstake Guide

How the askporter sweepstake works
  • Each person was randomly drawn 4 national teams at the start of the World Cup.
  • You win if one of your teams wins the final on 19 July — last team standing takes the prize.
  • Once a team loses a knockout match, they're OUT and no longer in the running for you.
  • We're now in the Round of 32 (last 32 teams) — every match is win-or-go-home. Draws go to extra time, then penalties.
  • You don't need to understand football — just cheer for the flag next to your name when their match is on!
